The methodology behind the work

Three frameworks run through every engagement. They work together: the first decides what to point AI at, the second makes the prompting reliable, the third makes the habit stick.

The AI Sweet Spot

A rubric for finding the tasks where AI actually delivers, scored on four properties: high repetition, language-heavy, low-to-medium stakes, and privacy-safe. It scales into an Impact x Suitability matrix for prioritizing across a whole function. This is the diagnostic that opens every audit and workshop.

Read the full rubric →

The 5 P’s of Prompting

The framework I designed so non-technical teams can consistently get useful output from AI tools:

  • Premise: what’s the situation?
  • Persona: what role should the AI take?
  • Pattern: what does good look like?
  • Purpose: what should it produce?
  • Presentation: what format and length?
