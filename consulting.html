The 5 P’s of Prompting

Methodology spine. A framework for non-technical teams to consistently get useful output from AI tools.

I developed this framework for corporate AI workshops. It’s the structure participants use during training and carry back to their daily work.

  • Premise — what’s the situation? Audience, goal, constraints.
  • Persona — what role should the AI take?
  • Pattern — what does good look like? Show one or two examples.
  • Purpose — what specifically should it produce?
  • Presentation — what format and length?
